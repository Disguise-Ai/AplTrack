# Statly Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 27, 2026*

## 1. Introduction

Statly ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Statly mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Account Information:**
- Email address (for authentication and account creation)
- User ID (automatically generated for your account)

**App Data:**
- Connected app analytics (downloads, revenue, subscriptions) from services you authorize
- AI chat conversation history
- Subscription status

**Technical Data:**
- Device type and operating system (for app functionality)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Authenticate your account and provide access to the app
- Display your app analytics and revenue data
- Provide AI-powered marketing and sales assistance
- Process your subscription
- Improve app performance and features

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase** - Authentication and data storage
- **RevenueCat** - Subscription management
- **Anthropic** - AI chat functionality

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data

## 8. Children's Privacy

Statly is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
