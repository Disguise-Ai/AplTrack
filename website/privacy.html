Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Introduction

Statly ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Statly mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication and account creation)
  • User ID (automatically generated for your account)

App Data

  • Connected app analytics (downloads, revenue, subscriptions) from services you authorize
  • AI chat conversation history
  • Subscription status

Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system (for app functionality)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your account and provide access to the app
  • Display your app analytics and revenue data
  • Provide AI-powered marketing and sales assistance
  • Process your subscription
  • Improve app performance and features

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Statly:

  • Supabase - Authentication and data storage
  • RevenueCat - Subscription management
  • Anthropic - AI chat functionality

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

Children's Privacy

Statly is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: support@dontpanic.digital
